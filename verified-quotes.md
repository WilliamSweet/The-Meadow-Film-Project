# Verified Quotes — Rooted Film Site

Every pull quote displayed on the site must have a T1 or T2 entry here before going live.
Add an entry in the same session the quote is added to the component.

**Tiers:**
- T1 — direct transcript, published book, or official written record
- T2 — reliable secondhand (verified interview, major outlet, on-the-record)
- T3 — paraphrase or unverified — DO NOT publish at T3
